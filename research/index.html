I have worked on multiple topics throughout my studies such as dynamics and vibrations, instabilites, fracture mechanics, design and fabrications. During my PhD I have focused on the processes modelling of textile reinforcements by providing mathematical models, numerical solutions and experimental methods. To do this, I have analytically studied laminates in thermal environments under in-plane compressive forces, and have developed nonlinear material models to identify the material behavior during forming processes. For the experiments, I have designed various experimental rigs to model and determine the out-of-plane and bi-axial behavior of formable and tailorable materials.
Parallel to my projects, I have worked significantly on various topics in collaboration with various groups and teams.
